Drush
=====

Drupal
------
Downloaden drupal

    drush dl drupal

Updaten drupal

    drush up drupal

Modules
-------
Downloaden

    drush dl <module>

Enablen

    drush en <module>

Updaten

    drush up

Uninstall

    drush pm-uninstall <module>


Informatie
----------

    drush pm-list

Cache
-----

    drush cc all
